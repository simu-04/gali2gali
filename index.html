About Us

We are definitely NOT a “not for profit” organisation but our aim is to bridge the gap between online and offline. The kind of innovation and agility you can provide via offline mode is beyond one can fathom.

It might seem like we are tackling E-comm by E-comm, but NO we are here for a simpler notion to eliminate a stereotype from common people’s minds, that online is mostly better, it's an effort to get them to connect effortlessly to their local services and vice versa by our most beloved tools:
A Smartphone and Internet.
